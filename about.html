About Us

PT Abadi Sentosa is a trusted and reliable company engaged in the distribution and supply of quality stationery products, catering to the needs of offices, educational institutions, and commercial businesses throughout Indonesia. With a strong commitment to customer satisfaction and efficiency, PT Abadi Sentosa offers a wide range of office supplies, writing instruments, paper products, and other essential stationery items to support daily operational needs. The company is dedicated to maintaining long-term relationships with clients by delivering consistent service, competitive pricing, and timely delivery.

Since 1998, our company has built a solid reputation for consistency, reliability, and personalized service. We take pride in fostering long-term partnerships with our clients by ensuring timely delivery, competitive pricing, and a strong focus on quality. Our decades of experience have positioned PT Abadi Sentosa as a key player in the stationery supply industry in Indonesia.
